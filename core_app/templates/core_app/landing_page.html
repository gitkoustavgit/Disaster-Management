How Asha Brings Hope to the Frontline

Request Help Instantly

Victims can use an interactive map to submit precise, geo-tagged requests for medical aid, food, or rescue, ensuring help arrives exactly where it's needed.

Coordinate Responders

Volunteers and admins get a real-time dashboard of all active requests. They can assign tasks and update statuses, creating an efficient and coordinated response.

Stay Informed & Safe

Using a high-speed MongoDB backend, admins can instantly broadcast critical safety alerts and updates to everyone, ensuring community-wide awareness.
